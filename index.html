Astrology

Astrology is the ancient science which describes the ebb and flow of the 'god' principle which shapes all human life and consciousness. -C. Harvey

Fire

The Fire Element is represented by the three astrological signs Aries, Leo, and Sagittarius.

Air

The Air element is represented by the three astrological signs Gemini, Libra, and Aquarius.

Water

The Water element is represented by the three astrological signs Cancer, Scorpio, and Pisces.

Earth

The Earth element is represented by the three astrological signs Taurus, Virgo, and Capricorn.
